🏛️ Faculty of Science

Our Departments

Explore the five departments under NASTES at The Polytechnic Ibadan and what each one offers.

All five departments below sit under the Faculty of Science at The Polytechnic, Ibadan, which was established in 1970. NASTES represents students across these departments.
⚛️
Physics with Electronics
HND 1HND 2

Combines core physics principles, mechanics, and electromagnetism with practical electronics, focusing on circuit design, instrumentation, and applied technology. Graduates typically move into electronics, telecommunications, instrumentation engineering, renewable energy, and technical education roles.

📄 Past Questions
🧬
Biology and Microbiology
HND 1HND 2

Covers general biology, microbiology, genetics, ecology, and laboratory techniques for studying living organisms at both macro and microscopic levels. Graduates go on to work in medical laboratories, food and water quality control, research institutes, agriculture, and public health.

📄 Past Questions
🧪
Applied Chemistry
HND 1HND 2

Focuses on chemical principles applied to industry, covering organic, inorganic, analytical, and industrial chemistry. Students gain hands-on laboratory experience relevant to manufacturing, quality control, and chemical analysis. Graduates work in industrial labs, quality assurance, oil and gas, and manufacturing.

📄 Past Questions
🔬
Science Laboratory Technology (SLT)
ND 1ND 2

A broad, practical programme covering laboratory techniques across biology, chemistry, and physics, training students as skilled laboratory technologists. It prepares graduates to run and maintain laboratories in schools, hospitals, research centres, and industry, and is often a foundation before specializing further.

📄 Past Questions
💊
Pharmaceutical Technology
ND 1ND 2

Covers the science behind drug formulation, dosage forms, pharmaceutical chemistry, and basic pharmacology, bridging chemistry and health sciences. Graduates typically find roles in pharmaceutical manufacturing, drug quality control, community and hospital pharmacy support, and health product distribution.
